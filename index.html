Barramundi

Barramundi is in Tilakroad which is in Rajahmundry Opposite to Barramundi restuarant there is aBigBazaar supermarket.

Directions for traveling to food location from the airport

  1. Airport that is closest to my food location is Rajiv Gandhi Intenational Airport
  2. After coming out from the airport take a cab to Complex
    1. From Complex take a bus to Rajahmundry complex bus stop
    2. From complex bus stop in Rajahmundry, take a cab to Tilak Road which is 10 minutes away from complex
  3. From Tilak Road take right and then we can see Big Bazaar, from there go straight and we get my Favourit food location which is Barramundi
